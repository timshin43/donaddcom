ABOUT US

There are a lot of stray animals in the modern world who need help. We believe that this problem could be resolved and this starts by taking small steps. Every second of your time has a value that could be converted into real money with the help of technology. This project simplifies the donation process by allowing users to watch short ads in order to raise money for animals. Spending just a couple of minutes watching videos on this website enables you to make a priceless contribution and save a pets` life. A small contribution from each individual will have a significant impact on a dog or cat who could survive because of YOU! You do not need to donate your money; just go to the page -> choose the project you like -> and watch up to 10 videos per day on the project`s page.

Let’s try to make this world a little bit better.
